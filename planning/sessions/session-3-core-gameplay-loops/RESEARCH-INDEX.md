## Key Research Findings

### Player Archetypes (from Eco + Paradox)
- **The Builder**: Construction, megaprojects
- **The Economist**: Trading, market optimization
- **The Politician**: Governance, law-making
- **The Environmentalist**: Conservation, ecology
- **The Engineer**: Automation, efficiency
- **The Socializer**: Community, diplomacy

### Session Lengths
- **15-minute**: Resource gathering, quick trades
- **2-hour**: Project completion, economic planning
- **Multi-session**: Political campaigns, crisis response

### UI/UX Patterns (from Paradox)
- Layered heatmaps for environmental data
- Predictive feedback (show effects before action)
- Progressive disclosure (complexity reveals gradually)
- Nested tooltips for deep information

## Progression Timeline
- **Week 1**: Survival, basic establishment
- **Week 2**: Specialization, town formation
- **Week 3**: Industry, meteor preparation
- **Week 4+**: Advanced tech, political complexity

## Open Questions for This Session
