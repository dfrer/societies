## Timeline Reality Check

### Industry Case Studies

| Game | Timeline | Key Lesson |
|------|----------|------------|
| **Space Engineers** | 4+ years for multiplayer | Start networking early |
| **Factorio** | 8+ years continuous refinement | Perfection takes time |
| **Eco** | 7+ years and ongoing | Scope creep is real |
| **Lessons** | - | Start multiplayer from day one |

### Critical Unknowns

| Unknown | Risk Level | Mitigation Strategy |
|---------|------------|---------------------|
| AI-to-human economic equivalence ratio | **High** | Prototype in Month 2 |
| Entity count thresholds (OOP → ECS) | **Medium** | Performance testing in Month 1 |
| Godot 4.x + C# performance ceiling | **Medium** | Benchmark early, monitor continuously |
| Law system complexity tolerance | **High** | Prototype in Month 3 |
| Player retention patterns | **High** | Alpha testing in Month 6 |

## Risk-First Development Approach

### Priorities (in order)

1. **Technical feasibility** before content
2. **AI validation** before deep balance
3. **Multiplayer from day one** (not later)
4. **Performance testing** at each milestone
5. **Player testing** before polish

### Go/No-Go Criteria

| Checkpoint | Criteria | Fallback |
|------------|----------|----------|
| **Month 1** | 20 TPS with 50 entities | Reconsider architecture |
| **Month 2** | AI agents trade effectively | Simplify AI economy |
| **Month 3** | Law system validates | Reduce complexity |
| **Month 4** | Engagement metrics positive | Iterate core loop |
| **Month 6** | Alpha retention >30% | Pivot or reduce scope |
