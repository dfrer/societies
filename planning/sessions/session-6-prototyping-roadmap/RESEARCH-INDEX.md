## Key Research Findings

### Timeline Reality Check
- Space Engineers: 4+ years for multiplayer
- Factorio: 8+ years continuous refinement
- Eco: 7+ years and still in development
- Lesson: Start multiplayer early, not later

### Critical Unknowns
1. AI-to-human economic equivalence ratio
2. Entity count thresholds (OOP → ECS)
3. Godot 4.x + C# performance ceiling
4. Law system complexity tolerance
5. Player retention patterns

### Prototype Sequence
- **Month 1**: World/Simulation (prove engine works)
- **Month 2**: AI + Economy (prove AI can trade)
- **Month 3**: Governance (prove laws work)
- **Month 4**: Progression + Threats (prove engagement)
- **Month 5**: Environment (prove pollution challenge)
- **Month 6**: Alpha (integrated test)

### Risk-First Approach
- Technical feasibility before content
- AI validation before deep balance
- Multiplayer from day one
