## Key Research Findings

### Law System Structure (from Eco)
- **Triggers**: When law activates (e.g., "when hunting")
- **Conditions**: Who/where it applies (e.g., "only hunters")
- **Actions**: What happens (prevent, tax, confiscate)
- Server-side validation (non-negotiable)
- Performance: Not checking every law every tick

### Constitutional Framework
- Three-phase enactment: Proposal → Debate → Vote
- Templates: Democracy, republic, council, dictatorship
- Amendment processes
- Checks and balances

### Political Engagement (from Paradox)
- Predictive law preview (show effects before voting)
- Interest group reaction preview
- Visual power indicators (clout bars)
- Faction formation mechanics

### Anti-Griefing
- Constitutional deadlock prevention
- Checks on elected officials
- Exit mechanisms (leaving bad governments)
- Reputation systems
