# Concept Interview Records

Store one record per substantial interview:

`YYYY-MM-DD-topic-slug.md`

Use [the interview template](../templates/INTERVIEW-TEMPLATE.md).

An interview record should preserve:

- the questions actually asked;
- the user's answers without silently strengthening them;
- synthesized Candidate decisions;
- contradictions and dependencies;
- unresolved questions; and
- explicit approval, revision, rejection, or deferral outcomes.

Chat history is useful context, but these records are the durable design memory.
